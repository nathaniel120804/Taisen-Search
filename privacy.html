Services Covered

This policy applies to all Taisen services, including:

  • The Taisen search engine
  • User accounts for personalization and saved preferences
  • Analytics tools that help us improve performance and reliability

Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate and improve our services.

1. Information You Provide

  • Account details: When you create a Taisen account, we collect your email address and password for login and security.
  • Optional profile settings: You may provide additional information such as a display name.

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Search logs: Search terms and technical data (e.g., timestamps) to improve performance and prevent misuse.
  • Device & usage data: Browser type, device type, operating system, and interactions with our services.
  • IP address: Used for fraud prevention, security, and service optimization.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Sensitive personal information such as race, religion, political views, financial details, or health data.
  • Your identity, unless you voluntarily create an account.

How We Use Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • Provide services: Process search queries and deliver relevant results.
  • Maintain & improve services: Detect outages, troubleshoot, and enhance reliability.
  • Develop new features: Analyze trends to build new functionalities.
  • Protect against misuse: Detect spam, abuse, or security threats.

Your Privacy Controls

You remain in control of your information:

  • Account settings: Update your personal details anytime.
  • Search history management: View or delete your searches in your account dashboard.
  • Data export: Request a copy of your Taisen account data.
  • Account deletion: Delete your account anytime; all associated data will be permanently removed.

Sharing of Information

Taisen does not share your personal data with third parties, except:

We may share non-identifiable, aggregated data (e.g., general search trends) for research and analytics purposes.
