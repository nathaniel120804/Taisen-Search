Security Overview

Comprehensive security practices protecting your applications and data

Our Security Commitment

Taisen is built with security as a foundational principle. We implement multiple layers of protection to ensure your applications and data remain secure.

Infrastructure Security

Secure hosting through Cloudflare and Netlify with DDoS protection, firewalls, and continuous monitoring.

Data Protection

End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest, with secure authentication via Firebase.

Access Control

Role-based access controls and multi-layered authentication to protect sensitive information.

Security Principles

The foundation of our security approach

Defense in Depth

Multiple layers of security controls provide redundant protection in case one layer fails.

Least Privilege

Users and systems only have access to the minimum resources necessary for their function.

Continuous Monitoring

Ongoing security monitoring and regular assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities.

Privacy by Design

Privacy considerations are integrated into our development process from the start.
