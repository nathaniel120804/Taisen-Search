Our Compliance Framework

Taisen is committed to maintaining compliance with relevant data protection regulations and implementing security best practices to protect our users' data.

GDPR

COMPLIANT

General Data Protection Regulation - European Union's data protection law

Key Implementations:

  • Data subject rights management
  • Lawful basis for processing
  • Data protection impact assessments
  • International data transfer safeguards

Philippine Data Privacy Act

COMPLIANT

Republic Act No. 10173 - Philippines' comprehensive data privacy law

Key Implementations:

  • Data privacy principles adherence
  • Security measures implementation
  • Data breach management procedures
  • Registration with NPC where required

Global Privacy Standards

ALIGNED

Adherence to international privacy principles and best practices

Key Principles:

  • Privacy by design and default
  • Data minimization
  • Purpose limitation
  • Transparency and accountability

Our Ongoing Commitment

We continuously monitor changes in data protection regulations and update our practices accordingly. Our commitment to compliance is integral to how we build and operate the Taisen platform, ensuring that we maintain the trust of our users worldwide.
